Ikigai
Café

Ikigai Café is a tranquil retreat where the art of Japanese tea culture meets the warmth of artisanal coffee. Named after the Japanese concept of Ikigai—the intersection of passion, purpose, and joy—this café invites you to pause, reflect, and savor the moment. From the rich aromas of freshly brewed matcha lattes to the soothing ambiance filled with minimalist decor, every detail is designed to help you discover your own sense of balance and happiness. Whether you’re indulging in a delicate pastry, sipping on a calming green tea, or simply enjoying the peaceful surroundings, Ikigai Café offers a space for mindfulness and connection, where the simple pleasures of life are celebrated in every cup.

Coffee Services
