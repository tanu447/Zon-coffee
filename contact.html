Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. Is the cafe suitable for both couples and group meetings? Yes, our cafe offers cozy corners for couples and spacious seating for groups, ensuring comfort and a great atmosphere for both.
  • 2. Do you offer private rooms for group meetings? Yes, we have private meeting rooms available upon request for groups who need a more quiet, focused space.
  • 3. Are there any quiet corners for couples? Absolutely! We have intimate seating areas, perfect for couples looking for a peaceful spot to relax and enjoy their time together.
  • 4. Is there free Wi-Fi for group work or casual browsing? Yes, we provide free high-speed Wi-Fi, ideal for group meetings or couples who want to work or browse together.
  • 5. Do you have a special menu for couples or romantic settings? We offer a selection of wines, desserts, and coffee blends that create a cozy and romantic atmosphere, perfect for couples.
  • 6. Is the cafe a good place for business meetings? Yes, our spacious and quiet environment makes it ideal for casual business meetings, with plenty of seating options and a calm atmosphere.
  • 7. Do you host events or group activities? We occasionally host events such as open mic nights, trivia, and workshops, perfect for groups looking for a fun, interactive experience.
