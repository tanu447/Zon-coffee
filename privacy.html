Privacy & Policy

At Ikigai Cafe, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We collect personal data, such as your name, contact information, and purchase history, only when necessary for processing your orders, providing services, or administering our loyalty points program. This information is used exclusively for internal purposes to enhance your experience with us and ensure smooth transactions. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties without your consent, except as required by law. We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information, ensuring that it remains safe and confidential. By using our services, you consent to our privacy practices as outlined in this policy. For any concerns or requests regarding your data, please don’t hesitate to contact us.


We collect personal information, such as your name, email, phone number, and purchase history, only when necessary for processing orders, providing services, or managing our loyalty program.

The information we collect is used to improve your experience, process transactions, communicate with you, and personalize offers or promotions based on your preferences.

Ikigai Cafe employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent. We may disclose data when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website and improve the performance of our online services.

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you have concerns or need assistance regarding your data, please contact us.

By using our services, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your personal information as described in this policy.

We reserve the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated on our website, and the updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please reach out to us via our contact details provided on the website.
